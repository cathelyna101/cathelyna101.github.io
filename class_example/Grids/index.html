Bio

Studying a double major in Creative Advertising and Interactive Media with a minor in Graphic Design in the Communication school at The University of Miami. Transferred from The School of Art Institute of Chicago and studied a year with a BFA in Visual Communications and Photography. Transferred in order to specialize and achieve a greater insight of communications. Have been interested in fine arts and design since high school, specializing in various mediums as well as product design. Interested in business as well, worked part time in an automotive and plastic manufacturer in Indonesia. Additionally, ran a small bakery and freelanced as a custom painter. .
